# Code Of Conduct

Mortar uses a simple professional conduct standard:

- Be direct and respectful.
- Critique code, design, evidence, and tradeoffs instead of people.
- Keep discussions technical and actionable.
- Do not harass, threaten, or abuse contributors.
- Do not derail issues or pull requests with unrelated arguments.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when conduct blocks productive project work.
